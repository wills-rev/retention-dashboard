The threshold tiles tell where attention buys the most retention leverage.

Active Average MRR is concentrated at the top. Billing is top-heavy, while Tigerpaw’s risk is more long-tail. The dashboard should assign different plays by tile.

Recommended play by tile

  • $10K+: executive save / renewal plans.
  • $5K–$10K: proactive health review and sponsor mapping.
  • $1K–$5K: AM playbook with product-risk flags.
  • Under $1K: automation, education, and churn pattern detection.

Why this matters

  • Top accounts explain revenue movement quickly.
  • Long-tail accounts explain repeatable process defects.
  • Tigerpaw needs the second lens; Billing needs the first.
